The Breath

Optimising the breath can truly change an individual’s quality of life, providing renewed energy and productivity, optimising rest, repair, sleep and digestion, boosting the immune system and recalibrating one’s whole outlook on life. Breathwork practice is an ancient eastern system, once considered esoteric is now being fully adopted by high performing organisations as a cutting-edge approach to enabling individuals and teams to realise their true potential / capacity. Breathwork practice is scientifically validated, and the breath is recognised as a direct reflection of and gateway to our physical state and nervous system, with a proven dynamic impact on all physiological systems (immune, digestive, cardiovascular, hormonal, inflammatory etc..). Breathwork practice can keep us in homeostasis. Transformational breathwork works at a deep subconscious level to reset and rebalance the nervous system and is a cutting-edge modality for mental health cleanliness.
